Odin's Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry

Who knew eating healthy could be so tasty!

This recipe will help you stay healthy while refusing to sacrifice taste.

Even the pickiest of eater's will love this dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Tsp canola oil
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 3/4 inch pieces
  • 3 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup thinly sliced onion
  • 2 Tsp minced fresh ginger
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 5 cups broccoli florets
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 Tbsp light brown sugar
  • 1/4 Tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 Tsp freshly ground black pepper
